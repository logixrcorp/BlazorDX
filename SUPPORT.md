# Support

BlazorDX is **free and open source (MIT)** — the entire library, forever. There are no
license keys and nothing in the code is gated behind a paid tier. The tiers below fund
continued development and serve teams that need guarantees the community channel can't
provide; they buy *people's time and commitments*, not access to the code.

> Replace the placeholders (`<…>`) below with your real contact, links, and pricing
> before publishing.

## Community — free

The default for everyone.

- **Issues & questions:** open a GitHub issue at `<github-org>/<repo>/issues`.
- **Discussions / how-tos:** `<github-org>/<repo>/discussions`.
- **Docs:** the in-app documentation site (`/docs` in the demo) and the guides.
- Best-effort, no response-time guarantee. Bug reports with a minimal repro get the
  fastest attention.
